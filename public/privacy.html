Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 5, 2026
01
Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Parlay Killer:

  • Email address and name when you create an account
  • Google account information (name, email, profile picture) if you sign in with Google
  • Usage data — which features you use and how often, to enforce plan limits
  • Payment information processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details directly
  • IP address and browser type for security and abuse prevention
  • Session data stored in cookies to keep you logged in
02
How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Parlay Killer service
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Enforce daily usage limits based on your plan
  • Send account-related emails (receipts, password resets) — no marketing emails without your consent
  • Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access
03
Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with sportsbooks, advertisers, or unrelated third parties.

We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Google — only if you choose to sign in with Google. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Railway — our hosting provider, who stores data on our behalf under their terms
04
Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing simscaslee@gmail.com.

Usage logs (analysis history, feature usage counts) are retained for 90 days and then automatically deleted.

05
Cookies and Sessions

We use session cookies only — a single cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is deleted when you sign out or when your session expires (30 days).

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar services.

06
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at simscaslee@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07
Children

Parlay Killer is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at simscaslee@gmail.com and we will promptly delete the account.

08
Contact
